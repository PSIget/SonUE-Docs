# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 09.11.2023

Welcome to https://s2ue.org/. Your privacy and data protection are a top priority for us. This Privacy Policy describes the types of information we may collect and process during your visit to our site and how we use this information.

## 1. Collection and Use of Information

**Analytics**: We utilize third-party analytics services such as Google Analytics and Vercel Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our site. These services collect information that helps us improve our website and the user experience. Data collected through Google Analytics and Vercel Analytics include, but are not limited to, details on pages visited, time spent on the site, and how users arrived at our site.

**Personal Data**: We do not collect personal data of our users, such as names, addresses, email addresses, or telephone numbers, ourselves. Any personal information you provide is collected and processed by the aforementioned external analytics services according to their privacy policies.

## 2. How Analytics Services Use Your Data

**Google Analytics** and **Vercel Analytics** use the collected information to provide us with analytical reports and usage statistics of our site. These services may use the collected information for their own purposes, including improving their products and offering personalized advertising. For more detailed information, we recommend reviewing the privacy policies of Google Analytics and Vercel Analytics.

## 3. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal information provided to the analytics services. Please contact these services directly to exercise your rights.

## 4. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify our Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the last updated date indicated.

## 5. Contact Information

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding our Privacy Policy, please contact us at admin@s2ue.org.
